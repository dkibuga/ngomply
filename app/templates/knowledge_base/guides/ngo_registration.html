NGO Registration Guide

Complete guide to registering an NGO in Uganda

Registration Process Overview

Note: This guide provides general information about the NGO registration process in Uganda. For personalized guidance, use the Registration Module in NGOmply.

Step 1: URSB Incorporation

Before registering with the NGO Bureau, you must first incorporate your organization with the Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB).

  1. Prepare Memorandum and Articles of Association
  2. Complete URSB Form 20 (Application for Registration)
  3. Complete URSB Form 18 (Notice of Situation of Registered Office)
  4. Pay prescribed fees
  5. Submit application to URSB

Step 2: NGO Bureau Registration

After incorporation with URSB, you must register with the National Bureau for Non-Governmental Organizations.

  1. Complete NGO Bureau Form A (Application for Registration)
  2. Obtain NGO Bureau Form D (Recommendation by DNMC)
  3. Complete NGO Bureau Form N (Notification of Executive Committee Members)
  4. Prepare Annual Work Plan and Budget
  5. Prepare Governance Structure Chart
  6. Prepare Statement on Funding Sources
  7. Prepare Statement on Staffing Requirements
  8. Pay prescribed fees
  9. Submit application to NGO Bureau

Step 3: Obtain Permit of Operation

After approval, collect your Certificate of Registration and Permit of Operation from the NGO Bureau.

Required Documents

  • Certificate of Incorporation from URSB
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association
  • Organization Constitution
  • Proof of Payment of Registration Fees
  • Minutes of the General Assembly that elected the Governing Body
  • Workplan and Budget
  • Organizational Structure
  • Recommendation from DNMC (Form D)

Fees

Registration fees vary depending on the type of NGO:

  • Local NGO: UGX 100,000
  • Foreign NGO: USD 150
  • International NGO: USD 350
  • Regional NGO: USD 250
