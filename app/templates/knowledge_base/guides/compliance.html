Compliance Guide

Guide to maintaining operational compliance for NGOs and CBOs in Uganda

Compliance Requirements Overview

Note: This guide provides general information about compliance requirements. For personalized guidance, use the Operational Compliance Module in NGOmply.

Annual Returns

All registered NGOs and CBOs must submit annual returns to the relevant authorities:

  • NGOs: Submit annual returns to the NGO Bureau by March 31st each year
  • CBOs: Submit annual returns to the District Community Development Office

Annual returns typically include:

  • Annual report of activities
  • Financial statements
  • Updated list of board/executive committee members
  • Updated workplan and budget

Tax Compliance

NGOs and CBOs must comply with tax regulations:

  • Register for Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • File monthly PAYE returns for employees
  • File annual income tax returns
  • Apply for tax exemptions where applicable

Governance Requirements

  • Hold regular board/executive committee meetings
  • Maintain minutes of all meetings
  • Hold annual general meetings
  • Maintain proper financial records
  • Adhere to the organization's constitution

Operational Requirements

  • Operate within the approved objectives and geographical area
  • Maintain proper documentation of all activities
  • Notify authorities of any changes in leadership or constitution
  • Display registration certificate at the organization's premises
  • Cooperate with monitoring visits from authorities
