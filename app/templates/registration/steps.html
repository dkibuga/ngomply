Registration Steps

Follow these steps to complete your {{ org_type }} registration

{% if org_type == 'NGO' %}

NGO Registration Process

Step 1: URSB Incorporation

Register your organization with the Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB)

  • Prepare Memorandum and Articles of Association
  • Complete URSB Form 20 (Application for Registration)
  • Complete URSB Form 18 (Notice of Situation of Registered Office)
  • Pay prescribed fees
  • Submit application to URSB

Step 2: NGO Bureau Registration

Register with the National Bureau for Non-Governmental Organizations

  • Complete NGO Bureau Form A (Application for Registration)
  • Obtain NGO Bureau Form D (Recommendation by DNMC)
  • Complete NGO Bureau Form N (Notification of Executive Committee Members)
  • Prepare Annual Work Plan and Budget
  • Prepare Governance Structure Chart
  • Prepare Statement on Funding Sources
  • Prepare Statement on Staffing Requirements
  • Pay prescribed fees
  • Submit application to NGO Bureau

Step 3: Obtain Permit of Operation

After approval, collect your Certificate of Registration and Permit of Operation

{% else %}

CBO Registration Process

Step 1: Prepare Required Documents

Prepare the necessary documents for CBO registration

  • Draft Constitution
  • Complete CBO Form K (Application for Registration)
  • Prepare Annual Work Plan and Budget
  • Prepare Governance Structure Chart
  • Prepare Statement on Funding Sources

Step 2: Submit to DNMC

Submit your application to the District Non-Governmental Monitoring Committee

  • Pay prescribed fees
  • Submit all required documents

Step 3: Obtain Registration Certificate

After approval, collect your CBO Registration Certificate
